STR = store register (word)
Consider the STR instruction. (PQR / Opcode reference / row 323)
There are two sub-categories:
• immediate — of the form
TR Rt, [Rn, #immed]
The immediate value refers to an optional, fixed-value offset.
• register — of the form
STR Rt, [Rn, Rm]
The offset is the value of a second register Rm.
