USAT — unsigned saturate
• USAT Rd, #imm5, Rn{, <shift>}
• Operation:
• let x = Rn with optional LS or ASR
• let s = #imm5
• Case 1: x > 25 - 1
Rd < 25 - 1 and set APSR[Q] flag
• Case 2: x < 0
Rd - O and set APSRIQ] flag
• Case 3: otherwise
Rd + x
