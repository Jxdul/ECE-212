Conditional execution
• In traditional assembly language ISAs, branching was the only instruction that
could be made conditional.
• In ARMV7-M assembly language (Thumb-2 instruction set), most instructions
can be executed conditionally. This is done using a special IT (if-then) instruc-
tion block.
• Some conditional branch instructions do not need the IT, as a condition code
is included in their instruction encoding.
