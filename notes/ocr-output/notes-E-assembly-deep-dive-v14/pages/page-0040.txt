Example - Negation
In ARM assembly language, there is no separate NEG (negation) instruction, so
how do we do this operation? Use RSB (reverse subtract) with
RSB
or
RSB
How does this work? Let's compare the RTL descriptions of SUB vs. RSB:
• RSB { S}
Rd, Rn, #const means:
LET imm32 = ThumbExpImm(i:imm3:imm8)
Rd - imm32 - Rn
• SUB {S}
Rd, Rn, #const means:
LET imm32 = ThumbExpImm(i: imm3: 1mm8)
Rd - Rn - imm32
