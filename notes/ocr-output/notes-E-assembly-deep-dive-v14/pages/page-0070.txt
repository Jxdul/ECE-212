MLA — multiply and accumulate
• (32-bit) + (32-bit) × (32-bit) = (32-bit)
• ! only lower 32 bits of product retained
• does not depend on signed/unsigned
• MLA Rd, Rn, Rm, Ra
Rd = (Ra + Rn × Rm) [31:0]
MLS — multiply and subtract
• (32-bit) - (32-bit) × (32-bit) = (32-bit)
• 1 only lower 32 bits of product retained
• does not depend on signed/unsigned
• MLS Rd, Rn, Rm, Ra
Rd = (Ra - Rn x Rm) [31: 0]
