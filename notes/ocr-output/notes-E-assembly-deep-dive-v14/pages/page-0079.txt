SBFX — signed bit field extract
•
SBFX
Rd, Rn, #Isb, #width
• Extracts from Rn any number of adjacent bits at any position, puts these into
least-significant bits of Rd, then sign-extends.
UBFX — unsigned bit field extract
• UBFX Rd, Rn, #Isb, #width
• Extracts from Rn any number of adjacent bits at any position, puts these into
least-significant bits of Rd, then zero-extends.
