E.6.6 Bit field operations
BFC — bit field clear
•
BFC
Rd, #Isb, #width
• Clears a portion of Rd of given width and position. Other bits of Rd are not
changed.
e.g.,
BFC
R6, #7,#3
BFI — bit field insert
•
BFI
Rd, Rn, #Isb, #width
• Copies the #width least-significant bits of Rn at position #Isb of Rd. Other bits
of Rd are not changed.
CLZ — count leading zeroes
•
CLZ
Rd, Rm
• Rd - number of leading binary Os in Rm before first binary 1 is seen
