Example # 3 — Passing parameters, one more time
Write a subroutine to add two 64-bit integers P and Q. The subroutine should be
AAPCS-compliant.
As before, P = PH: PL and Q = QH: QL. The two input values can be passed in
registers RØ to R3 and the result will be returned in R0 and R1.
