Key idea for stacks
Stack operations are FILO:
• When we push items onto the stack, we must pop the items off in reverse
order.
• If we push k items onto the stack, we need to pop k items off the stack by
the end of the subroutine, to ensure the stack is the same size (i.e., that SP
) at the beginning and end of the subrou-
tine.
