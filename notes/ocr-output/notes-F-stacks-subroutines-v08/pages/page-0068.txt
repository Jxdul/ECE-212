F.6 Program design process
Without a proper design process, jumping straight into coding leads to poor results.
A good design process is based on:
• top-down design
• modularity
• structured programming
• testability
• recoverability
F.6.1 Top-down design
Top-down design is a method to handle large, complex problems in a systematic
way:
• decomposing the problem into smaller, more tractable pieces
• separation of goals and means
