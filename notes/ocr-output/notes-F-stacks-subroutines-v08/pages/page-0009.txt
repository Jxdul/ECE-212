F.4.2 Additional stack terminology
• top of the stack
• when we push an item onto the stack — the new item goes onto the top
of the stack
• when we pop an item from the stack — the item is removed from the top
of the stack
• For CPUs, we initialise an
stack as a region within memory, with stack
origin at the top.
• A stack pointer (SP) keeps track of the top of the stack.
