STM32L432xx microcontroller: So many features! *°
Includes:
• ARM Cortex-M4 core with FPU, DSP, MPU
• 256 kB Flash memory, 64 kB SRAM
• ADC, 2x comparators, op amp, 2x DAC
• RTC, 32-bit timer, PWM timer, 4x 16-bit timer, 2x low-power timer
• 3x capacitive sensing channels
• 2x DMA
• 2x I2C
• 2x SPI
• 2x USART, low-power UART
• CAN
• USB
• SWPMI
Ref: STM microcontroller datasheet
