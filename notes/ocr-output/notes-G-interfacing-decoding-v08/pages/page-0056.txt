G.9 Summary
Here, we studied computer interfacing; in particular, how the CPU communicates
with other parts of the computer:
• The STM32L432KC is a microcontroller, a CPU integrated with a lot of other
sub-systems
• Communication between the CPU and main memory is a crucial operation
• Devices are mapped to memory addresses, allowing facile interfacing
• Address decoding determines which external device is selected
Up next, exceptions and interrupts:
• how does the microprocessor deal with something that needs its immediate
attention, disrupting normal, "linear" processing of instructions?
